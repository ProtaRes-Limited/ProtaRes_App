5. Responder Tiers and Credentials

Responders are classified into four tiers based on verified qualifications:

  • Tier 1 — Active Healthcare Professional: Current GMC, NMC, or HCPC registration required.
  • Tier 2 — Retired Healthcare Professional: Former professional registration plus completion of a refresher course.
  • Tier 3 — First Aid Trained: Current first aid certificate from a recognised provider (e.g., St John Ambulance, British Red Cross).
  • Tier 4 — Community Witness: No qualifications required. Limited to scene safety, video streaming, and relaying instructions.

You must only provide genuine credentials. Submitting false or fraudulent credentials is a criminal offence and will result in immediate account termination and referral to the relevant authorities.

Your credential tier determines which emergencies you are alerted to and which interventions you are authorised to perform within the App. You must not exceed the scope of your tier.
