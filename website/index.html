What is ProtaRes?

ProtaRes connects trained emergency responders to nearby incidents using predictive trajectory-based alerting. Unlike traditional radius-based systems, ProtaRes predicts where responders will be and alerts them accordingly — reducing time-to-first-response and saving lives.

Key Features

Corridor Algorithm

Predicts responder trajectories to alert those heading toward emergencies.

Green Badge Verification

Real-time credential verification via dynamic QR codes for emergency services.

Witness Mode

Live video streaming from emergency scenes to dispatchers and responders.

Multi-Agency Coordination

Seamless integration with ambulance, police, and fire services.

Built for the NHS Ecosystem

ProtaRes is designed to integrate with NHS Ambulance Trusts and emergency services across England. The platform complies with NHS DTAC, DCB0129/DCB0160 clinical safety standards, UK GDPR, and WCAG 2.2 Level AA accessibility requirements.

Four-Tier Responder System

Responders are classified by qualification: Tier 1 (active healthcare professionals), Tier 2 (retired healthcare workers), Tier 3 (first aid certified), and Tier 4 (community witnesses). Each tier has defined permitted interventions to ensure safe, appropriate care.
